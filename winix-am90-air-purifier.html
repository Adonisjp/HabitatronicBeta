What is the WINIX AM90 Air Purifier and What Does It Do?

Powerful and effective, the WINIX AM90 Air Purifier is meant to clear allergens and dangerous pollutants from the air in your house. It gathers dust, pollen, pet hair, smoke, smells, and even minute particles like viruses and bacteria using a mix of cutting-edge filtration systems. Cleaner, healthier air produced as a result can enable you to feel better and breathe easier.

Features

  • 5-Stage Air Purifier: Pre-filter, carbon filter, HEPA filter, PlasmaWave technology, and an ionizer make up the 5-stage air purification system found in the AM90 This all-encompassing technology guarantees the capture and neutralizing of many kinds of pollutants.
  • Smart Sensors: Real-time monitoring of the air quality via smart sensors included in the purifier To guarantee best performance and energy economy, it automatically modulates the fan speed to correspond with the current pollution levels.
  • Sleep Mode: The sleep mode lowers the lights and sounds, therefore establishing a quiet surroundings for relaxation.
  • Air Quality Indicator: A clear air quality indicator shows the present air quality level, therefore enabling simple monitoring of the purifier's performance.
  • Filter Replacement Indicator: The purifier guarantees always maximum performance by reminding you when it is time to replace the filters.

Usability

User-friendliness is considered in design of the WINIX AM90. The filter replacement procedure is simple and the settings are easy to operate. Particularly in sleep mode, the purifier is also rather silent, which qualifies for use in living rooms and bedrooms.
