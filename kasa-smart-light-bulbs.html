What are Kasa Smart Light Bulbs and What Do They Do?

Kasa Smart Light Bulbs are not like other light bulbs. These clever lighting solutions link to the Wi-Fi network of your house so that you may use smart assistants like Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant to remotely control them by voice commands or smartphone. From the comfort of your couch or perhaps even when you're not home, you can turn lights on or off, dim them, change colors, and even create routines.

Features

  • Remote Control: Use the Kasa app to manage your lights from anywhere.
  • Dimming: Change brightness to produce the ideal atmosphere.
  • Color Changing: Choose among millions of hues to complement your d√©cor or mood.
  • Scheduling: Plan timers and schedules for automatic illumination adjustments.
  • Voice Control: Works hands-free with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant under voice control.

Usability - A Bright Idea

Kasa Smart Light Bulbs are easy to set up. Download the Kasa app, just screw them into regular light sockets, and follow the basic directions. The easy-to-use layout of the app helps you to navigate and change your lighting choices.
