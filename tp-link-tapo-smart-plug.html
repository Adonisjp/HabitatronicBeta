Your Gateway for Effortless Smart Home Control: TP-Link Tapo Smart Plug

Comprising a small gadget plugging into a conventional electrical outlet, the TP-Link Tapo Smart Plug It turns your regular appliances—lamps, fans, coffee makers, etc.—into smart gadgets you may operate from a distance using your smartphone or voice commands. The wonder of a smart plug is realizing you could turn off your bedroom lights without leaving your bed or timing your morning coffee to start before you wake up.

Features

  • Remote Control: Available for iOS and Android, the Tapo app allows you to remotely operate your connected-in devices from anywhere with an internet access.
  • Voice Control: Works perfectly for hands-free operation with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant.
  • Scheduling: Making timers and schedules will help you to automatically run your appliances.
  • Away Mode: Turn lights on and off at random to simulate someone's house while you're away.
  • Compact Design: Does not impede next outlets.
  • Overload Protection: Automatically cuts off power if the current rises above the allowed level.

1. Outlet the Tapo Smart Plug into.
2. Get the Tapo app and register an account.
3. Connect the plug to your Wi-Fi network according in-app directions.
4. Start under manage your gadgets.
