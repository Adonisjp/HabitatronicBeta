What is it and for what?

The Yamiry Fingerprint Lock is a smart lock unlocking your door with fingerprint technology. With up to 20 fingerprints stored, you can distribute access to friends and relatives. It also features a crucial backup should you forget your fingerprint or the batteries run out.
One excellent approach to increase the security of your house is using the Yamiry Fingerprint Lock. Install and operate it easily; it will help to keep your family and possessions safe.

Features

  • Fingerprint Unlock: Easily unlock using your fingerprint, up to twenty fingerprints.
  • Key Backup: With a physical key backup, peace of mind.
  • Easy DIY Install: Fits most doors and installs in minutes.
  • App Control (Optional): Manage lock remotely with the TTlock app; this requires separate purchase.
  • Voice Control (Optional): Alexa will unlock with your voice; this requires Yamiry Gateway.
  • Auto-Lock: Automatically locks behind you for convenience.

Usability

The Yamiry Fingerprint Lock is very easy to use. Just lay your finger on the fingerprint sensor to open your door. The lock will rapidly scan your fingerprint and, should it match, the door will open.
Should you be using the key backup, just slide the key into the lock to unlock the door.


Why Buy the Yamiry Fingerprint Lock

There are many reasons to buy this Smart Lock. Here are just a few:

Improved security: Your house's security could be much enhanced with the Yamiry Fingerprint Lock. One other safe approach to unlock your door is by fingerprint recognition instead of a conventional key.
Convenience: Using the Yamiri Fingerprint Lock is quite handy. You have no need to worry about losing or carrying around keys.
Peace of mind: Knowing that your house is safe will help you to relax using the Yamiry Fingerprint Lock.
